Privacy Policy

App: PDF Text Editor & Converter · Developer: Armitor Apps
Effective date: 23 June 2026

The short version

This app edits PDFs entirely on your device. The PDF files you open are processed locally and are never uploaded to us — we operate no servers and never see, store, or transmit your documents. The only data leaving your device comes from third-party services we use to keep the app free (ads) and to sell the optional one-time upgrade (payments), described below.

Information we collect

We, the developer, do not collect any personal data. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and your documents, edits, and saved projects stay on your device (in the app's local storage). We have no analytics of our own.

Third-party services

To keep the app free and to offer the optional upgrade, the app uses these Google services, which may collect data under their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — shown to free users. To serve ads, AdMob may collect device information, app-usage signals and advertising identifiers. See Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data. Buying the one-time Pro upgrade removes ads.
  • Google Play Billing — used only if you buy the Pro upgrade. The purchase is handled by Google; we do not receive or store your payment details.

Permissions

  • Internet — required to load ads and to use the system share sheet when saving/exporting.
  • Files you choose — the app only accesses the PDF and image files you explicitly open or save. It does not scan your device.

Data sharing & selling

We do not sell your data and we do not share your documents with anyone. Ad-related data is handled by Google AdMob under its policies, not by us.

Children

This app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Your choices

  • You can limit ad personalization in your device's Settings → Google → Ads (e.g. reset or delete your advertising ID).
  • Buying the one-time Pro upgrade removes ads entirely.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored documents, edits and projects.

Data security

Because your documents never leave your device, they are protected by your device's own security. We hold no copies to secure or breach.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.
